This year, we are excited to offer two tracks for participants to choose from below tracks:

  1. Test automation for E-learning Mobile App (Socratic)
  2. Intelligent mobile Chatbot (Wysa)


For Track 1, the E-learning Mobile App track, and for Track 2, the Intelligent Mobile Chatbot track, the top three teams in each track will be recognized with cash prizes and certificates.

The awards for each track are as follows:

  1. First place team: Certificate + $5000 cash reward
  2. Second place team: Certificate + $3000 cash reward
  3. Third place team: Certificate + $1000 cash reward

These awards are designed to recognize the hard work and innovation of the top-performing teams in each track. We encourage all participants to bring their best work to the challenge, and to take advantage of the workshops and presentations offered throughout the event to develop their skills and learn from other experts in the field.
